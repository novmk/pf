Advent Calendar-like Web Portfolio
Digital, Interaction, Coding, 2023

The web portfolio typically serves as a platform showcasing a designer's interests, preferences, and body of work. As the end of the year approached, an Advent calendar was utilised as a visual metaphor to celebrate the year-end. An advent calendar traditionally consists of numbered compartments, each concealing small gifts or treats. Drawing inspiration from this form as a graphic motif, the website has represented personal interests and works within boxes marked with numbers. By concealing elements within these numbered boxes, akin to the conventional structure of an advent calendar, the website invites users to explore and discover the contents by clicking on each box.

Feel free to click on the boxes to unveil what is inside.
