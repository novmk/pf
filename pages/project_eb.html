Estuary Brands
Brand Identity, 2021

Estuary Brands, a global brand licensing company based in Seoul, sought a branding strategy that would instill confidence in clients while presenting a modern image. The chosen design features a typeface with varying thickness in its sans-serif font and a primary colour palette dominated by a dark turquoise, conveying a modern and trustworthy image.
