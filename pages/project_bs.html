Butter Shower
Brand Identity, Art Direction, Digital, … , 2021

In light of extensive market research conducted in South Korea, discernible demand has been identified for ingredient-centric products positioned within the Masstige category. Consequently, an initiative has been launched to develop a line of products with butter as the key ingredient, with the overarching goal of establishing a brand renowned for offerings imbued with exceptional moisturising properties. The project proposal advocates for the establishment of a brand targeted primarily at individuals with dry skin, with a strategic emphasis on an online retail platform and the cultivation of a distinct brand identity, thereby ensuring a premium and cohesive consumer experience.
